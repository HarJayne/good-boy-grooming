About Us

Good Boy Grooming Studios has been caring for your furry friends since it's establishment in 2006.


We offer a number of top notch services to ensure your beloved pet is always looking, and feeling, their best.


We cater to any and all breed size and have extensive knowledge on how to care for those pooches who are getting on in years or who may typically have some difficulty in a grooming environment. To enquire further about our range of services please contact us. Alternatively, to book an appointment for your dog please fill out an appointment form on the next page.

Puppy's First Groom

There are many dogs who find the grooming process stressful. Often, this is because the first groom was a frightening experience. Here at Good Boy Grooming Studios we offer a Puppy's First Groom special to make that first grooming experience a positive one.


The Puppy's First Groom package takes extra time for your pup to acclimate to the grooming studio and all its sounds and smells. The Puppy's First Groom package also includes the use of our whisper blow dryers and Puppy skin friendly shampoos
